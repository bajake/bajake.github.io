The Smartest People in the (Internet Chat) Room

Mar 12th, 2013

Always work with the smartest people you can find, and always be in a little over your head.

Marissa Mayer, CEO of Yahoo New York Magazine Interview

I take this advice to heart everyday and do my best to surround myself with smart people. I also like to follow and read people who not only inspire me but are some of the smartest and most successful tech people in the world. Below is a short list of people who I enjoy right now.

  • Jason Fried – Building products and cultures at 37Signals, a company that is bootstrapped, profitable and proud.
  • Jeff Bezos – The true king of the long term company strategy.
  • Brad Feld – Managing director of Boulder based Foundry Group.
  • Tina Roth Eisenberg – Super blogger known as swissmiss and a testament that side projects can lead to great businesses.
  • Bijan Sabet – Great photographer and general partner at Spark Capital.
  • Jack Dorsey – Co-founder of Twitter and CEO of Square. He understands that technology can be beautiful.
  • Tony Hsei – Tony takes company culture very seriously. It paid off when Amazon bought Zappos for $1 billion.
  • Mark Benoiff – Pioneered the 1-1-1 business model and runs Salesforce.com, one of the most innovative companies in the world.
  • Chris Dixon – Entrepreneur turned VC at Andersson Horowitz.
  • Aaron Levie – Humor and hard work pay off for the CEO of Box.com.
  • Elon Musk – He is the inspiration for Tony Stark in the Iron Man movies for good reason. PayPal, SpaceX, Tesla and Solar City.

This list would not be complete without Steve Jobs so I will end with one of my favorite quotes. This is from his father and influenced him the rest of his life.

When you’re a carpenter making a beautiful chest of drawers, you’re not going to use a piece of plywood on the back, even though it faces the wall and nobody will ever see it. You’ll know it’s there, so you’re going to use a beautiful piece of wood in the back. For you to sleep well at night, the aesthetic, the quality, has to be carried all the way through.

Who inspires you on a daily basis?
